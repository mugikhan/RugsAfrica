What information does Rugsafrica.com collect?

In order to serve you better, Rugsafrica collects and stores your personal information when you: (i) create an account (ii) sign up to be notified of special deals like discounts and exclusive offers; or (iii) enter a contest or promotion on our site. An account is created when you do one of following things: make a purchase on Rugsafrica, create a wish list on Rugsafrica , or register for an account on Rugsafrica, or enter certain Rugsafrica sweepstakes. When creating an account, we ask you for your first and last name, your e-mail address, a password, and perhaps some other similar information. Our system also may gather information about the areas you visit on our site as well as the IP address you use to connect to our site..

Your information is used to process your orders and personalize your shopping experience on Rugsafrica, for internal purposes and to comply with any legal requirements. Of course, you always have the option to opt-out from receiving such offers (for instructions on how to opt-out, please see below).

How does Rugsafrica.com safeguard my personal information?

Rugsafrica has implemented a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. How does Rugsafrica.com use my e-mail address?

Your e-mail address is used to create and identify your account on Rugsafrica. If you would rather not receive this information, or would like to opt-out of receiving e-mail of this kind, simply click on the “Unsubscribe” link that appears at the bottom of any email and follow the simple instructions to have your email address removed from the list of customers who receive notices of new functionality, services, special offers and promotions.
